Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Who is this for? Do I need to be experienced? ▼
This is for any mortgage broker who wants to do more commercial deals — whether you're brand new to commercial or already doing a few deals a year and want to level up. If you've got a deal (or a potential deal) and you're not sure how to structure it, position it, or which lender to approach, this is for you.
Do I need commercial experience to start? ▼
No. Many brokers come with zero commercial experience — just a deal they don't want to lose. The sessions are designed to meet you where you are. We'll work through your actual deal together, and you'll learn by doing.
What happens in a session? ▼
We review your real deal together — the borrower, the security, the numbers, the story. I'll show you how a bank credit analyst would assess it, identify the weak spots, and help you structure the submission so it gets approved. You'll leave with a clear action plan and specific next steps.
How do I prepare for a session? ▼
Run your deal through the free Deal Triage™ tool first — it takes 5 minutes and gives us a starting point. If you have financials, a security address, or loan amount in mind, bring those too. The more real information you have, the more practical the session will be.
Are sessions online or in-person? ▼
All sessions are online via video call (Zoom or Google Meet). This keeps things flexible — you can book from anywhere in Australia. If you're Sydney-based and prefer in-person, reach out and we can discuss.
What if my deal gets declined anyway? ▼
Declines happen — even to experienced commercial bankers. The goal isn't to guarantee approval; it's to make sure you submit the strongest possible application and understand exactly why if it doesn't get up. That knowledge compounds — you'll be better at every deal after this one.
Can I get a refund? ▼
Sessions are non-refundable once delivered. However, if you need to reschedule, just give 24 hours' notice and we'll move it. If for any reason you feel a session didn't deliver value, reach out — we'll make it right.
How is this different from PD days? ▼
PD days teach theory to a room of 50 people. This is 1-on-1, using your actual deal, with someone who's sat on the other side of the desk assessing these exact applications. You won't learn "what is an ICR" — you'll learn "why your deal's ICR won't pass and what to do about it."
Do you help with the actual submission? ▼
I don't submit on your behalf — you're the broker and it's your client relationship. But I will help you structure the deal, write the credit narrative, choose the right lender, and anticipate the questions credit will ask. Think of it as having a banker in your corner.
Can I book just one session to try? ▼
Absolutely. The Standard 1v1 ($399 + GST) is designed exactly for that — one deal, one session, real outcomes. No commitment required. Most brokers come back because the ROI speaks for itself.
